The collection

Twenty-plus Porsches.
All of them driven.

This is a working collection. Every car in it has been driven. Most have been driven hard.

Members encounter these cars at warehouse events, dinners, and track days throughout the year. They are not behind rope lines or on static display. They are present the way a car should be. Available, not protected.

The collection currently sits at 20-plus Porsches spanning four decades of the brand. The list changes. Cars come and go. What stays consistent is the standard: mechanically significant, historically relevant, or built with real intention. Often all three.

Ownership history

Forty-plus cars over time.

The current collection represents where things stand now. Getting here took years and somewhere north of 40 cars.

The significant ones come up in conversation at events: the Carrera GTs from earlier in the decade, the rare 911 variants that moved through before the current lineup took shape, the cars that informed the standard the collection holds today. Not all of them are listed here. That is not the point of this page.

The point is that the cars present now were chosen the same way everything was chosen: driven, significant, or both. The history behind the collection is what makes that standard credible.

Membership puts you around this collection at events throughout the year.
