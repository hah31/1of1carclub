1 of 1 Car Club

A private club built around rare Porsches.

For people who want in on the cars and the community behind them.

Chicago. Dallas. Las Vegas. Los Angeles. Palm Beach. Scottsdale.

Apply to join

The cars that matter in Porsche's history: the air-cooled 911s, the GT cars, the race-winning 935s. There's no new supply. The people who understood that early are not selling.

1 of 1 Car Club exists around one of the deepest private collections of these cars in the country. Members drive them at track days and events. Members who want to go further can hold fractional ownership in cars from the collection. Privately, directly, by arrangement.

We're not a car show club. We're not a track day service. This is a community of people who take these cars seriously, as machines and as assets.

What this is
  • Track days at G2 Motorsports Park in Dallas.
  • Drives in Chicago, Los Angeles, Palm Beach, and Scottsdale worth actually making.
  • Warehouse dinners and events with the cars present, not roped off.
  • An introduction to one of the most significant private Porsche collections in the country.
  • A private path to fractional ownership in cars from that collection.
  • People who already know why a 993 GT2 R exists and what it cost to build one.
What this isn't
  • A concours.
  • A networking event with cars as props.
  • A public fund or a trading app.
  • Somewhere to park your car and have someone photograph it.

G2 Motorsports Park

G2 Motorsports Park is our home track in Texas. It's purpose-built and properly fast. The facility handles track car storage for members who want a dedicated car in Dallas.

Track days at G2 are the anchor event for this club. Not the whole picture, but the thing that separates people who drive from people who talk about driving.

We run a calendar of track events throughout the year. If you want to be on that calendar, apply.

The Drives and Dinners

Chicago

The Chicago calendar runs drives and dinners. Routes worth the drive, not just an excuse to move in a group. Warehouse events that put you around the cars and the people.

Dallas

Dallas is anchored by G2. Track days, car storage, and events at the park. If you're Texas-based and want a home for a track car, this is the infrastructure for it.

Los Angeles. Las Vegas. Palm Beach. Scottsdale.

Active calendars across all four markets. Drives and dinners built around the same standard as Chicago and Dallas. If you're in one of these cities, apply and we'll tell you exactly where things stand.

The collection.

The founder has been buying, driving, and racing Porsches for decades. The current collection runs over 20 cars. A 993 GT2 R. Several DP Motorsport builds. Two cars in active film production for American Speed, an Amazon MGM film starring Tom Holland and Austin Butler.

Members see these cars at events. Not through glass.

For members who want more than access, there's a path to ownership. That conversation happens after you've applied.
