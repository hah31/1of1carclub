1 of 1 Car Club

A private club for people who drive their Porsches.

Chicago and Dallas. Members by invitation.

Apply to join

1 of 1 Car Club is for serious Porsche people. The ones who know the difference between a 964 RS and a 964 Carrera, who think a 996.1 GT3 is one of the most underrated 911s ever made, and who would rather put miles on a car than put it behind glass.

We're based in Chicago and Dallas. We run track days at G2 Motorsports Park. We do drives, warehouse dinners, and events built around one of the deepest private Porsche collections in the country.

If that sounds like where you want to be, apply below.

What this is
  • Track days at G2 Motorsports Park in Dallas.
  • Drives through the Chicagoland area worth driving.
  • Dinners and events at the warehouse.
  • Time around rare Porsches — air-cooled, analog, used.
  • Real friendships with people who actually care about these cars.
What this isn't
  • A gala.
  • A concours.
  • A place to polish cars and look at them.
  • Networking.
  • Status signaling.

G2 Motorsports Park

G2 Motorsports Park is our home track in Texas. It's purpose-built and properly fast. The facility handles track car storage for members who want a dedicated car in Dallas.

Track days at G2 are the anchor event for this club. Not the whole picture, but the thing that separates people who drive from people who talk about driving.

We run a calendar of track events throughout the year. If you want to be on that calendar, apply.

The Drives and Dinners

Chicago

The Chicago calendar runs drives and dinners. Routes that are worth the drive, not just an excuse to move in a group. Warehouse events that put you around the cars and the people. Evenings that go longer than planned because the conversation is good.

Dallas

Dallas is anchored by G2. Track days, car storage, and events at the park. If you're Texas-based and want a home for a track car, this is the infrastructure for it.

Both calendars are active. Both communities are real.

We're building toward additional markets. Nothing to announce yet. If you're not in Chicago or Dallas, apply anyway — we'll tell you where things stand.

The collection.

The founder's collection runs over 20 Porsches. Multiple Carrera GTs. A 993 GT2 R. Several DP Motorsport builds. Two cars currently in production for a film called American Speed.

Members see these cars at events throughout the year. Not behind rope lines.
